About Us

A trusted transport partner with 12 years of experience on the roads of Albania and the Balkans.

Who we are

EL TRANS is an Albanian company specialising in freight transport, based in Elbasan. For more than 12 years we have delivered safe, on-time transport solutions for businesses operating across Albania, the Balkans and Western Europe.

With a modern fleet — including refrigerated vehicles and high-volume trailers of up to 120m³ — we handle every requirement, from small consignments to specialised loads.

Our mission is simple: your goods arrive safe, intact and on time, every time you trust us with them.
